About me

I am a full stack web developer and a licensed physical therapist. I've worked in the clinics for about three years before pursuing coding. I started my coding journey when I underwent an IT Coding Bootcamp at Tuitt Philippines Inc.

My skills include HTML5, CSS, Javascript/jQuery, PHP and MySQL. I also have experience with Bootstrap and Materialize for front-end frameworks and Laravel for back-end frameworks. Other skills include JSON and AJAX.

My work

ShortFlix

Background:

I love watching short films and for our second capstone we were tasked to create something that would implement CRUD on our website. So I thought I'd make a short film repository.

As a user, I can add, edit and delete videos and visitors can view them. Video embedding was done by using the url to get both thumbnails and video through API's. This was made purely with PHP and MySQL as we haven't tackled object-oriented programming at this point.

Boyd's Anatomy

Background:

Boyd's Anatomy was my first capstone after learning the front-end languages. Learning resources online always bugs me a bit because some layouts are messy and distracting so with my first capstone I thought I'd make a learning resource website that is clean and easy to read. All the content was made by me since I also have a background with teaching.

I used Materialize as my framework for this website just to test out a different framework from the one we used in our class. The color scheme was based on what colors influence learning.

Interests

Background:

Interests is a simple take on social network with the premise of finding people based on similar interests. It's made using Laravel as its framework.

The user can post their thoughts as well as upload photos. They can follow people and people can follow them back. The design is minimalist to evoke simplicity.
